Up

A water tower project looking at creating an architecture that pushes into the realms of paper architecture but also investigates just how much detail is required to create a suspension disbelief.

Diagrams

Initially the water tower took form through the usage of traditional means of construction. The strictly vertical elements would then later be stripped away so that the structure would enter the realm of the implausible.

Inspiration

With an ideal formed, the structure slowly makes it’s way back towards reality, taking elements inspired by Salvadore Dali’s “The Burning Giraffe” while at the same time avoiding the usage of stricty vertical elements.
