Hey there. You can call me Moe

I'm a Multidisciplinary Designer

What does that even mean?

I'm an architect, artist, designer, guitarist, and a vocalist. If it's remotely artistic in nature I involve myself with it.

Am I an architect? Or am I a digital designer? What exactly do I do?

I am a UX Designer

But in the broader sense.

My focus is on what makes the user smile, or better yet - improve the quality of living of the user, whoever the user is - be it a person occupying a room, walking in a public space, strolling through a park, sitting on that park bench, examining a poster, reading a book, or using an application on the phone.

I believe that at it's core:

Design is Innovative Problem Solving

...and making things work better.

And that's what I'm about! Throw me into any field and, I'll use design thinking to tackle and solve problems, and then turn them into new opportunities.

The works I've featured here are all under a single thematic umbrella of experience design to demonstrate that:

Design Thinking is Universal.

The only real difference in the design process is the nature of the final product.
