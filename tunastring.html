Tuna String

Tuna String is a digital guitar tuner app that will be available on both the Android and iOS. This app is for guitarists of all levels that want a quick no non-sense tune up so that they can get jamming as quick as possible with no fuss.

Tuna String’s Pluck-N-Play interface all you have to do is just open the app, pick a tuning key, and pluck away while Tuna String tells whether you need to tigthen, or loosen the strings.

Ideation

Mood Board

In order to get an idea over the look and feel of this application, a mood board was compiled. The application is meant to be simple and quick to use with no fuss about it. The feel I was going for was a more laid back one compared to existing guitar tuners in the app market, which tend to focus too much on looking like a physical tuner. I wanted this to be an app any beginner can download, and use right on the spot without have to learn how to read a tuner.

Typography and Colour

The overall mood for the app needed to be casual as to not to drive off new guitarists, but at the same time deliver the information efficiently and clearly. The typefaces set for the application were Fredoka as the primary type, and Opens Sans for information deliverance.
